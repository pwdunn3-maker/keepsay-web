Common questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Keepsay subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). To cancel, go to your device's subscription settings and select Keepsay.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion by emailing us at hello@stubborngood.co. We'll permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

How do I export my memories?

In the app, go to Profile → Export your data. You can export all your memories at any time, regardless of your subscription status.

What happens to my memories if I stop subscribing?

Your memories are always yours. If your subscription ends, you move to read-only mode — you can view and export everything, but won't be able to create new memories until you resubscribe.

How do I redeem a gift code?

In the app, go to Profile → Enter a gift code, and enter your 6-character code. If you have trouble, email us and we'll help.
